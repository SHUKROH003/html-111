Jollof Spaghetti

A Nigerian pasta dish This meal is perfect for family dinners and can be prepared with simple ingredients you may already have at home.

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 5 tbsp groundnut oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 table spoonfulls of tomato paste
  • 400g beef chicken gizard
  • 2 balls of fresh tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 pcs of bayleaf
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 table spoofulls of curry
  • 1 table spoofulls of thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • Grounded dry pepper
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Cook your spag for few minutes with some salt.
  2. Pour spag in a sieve and let the water drain off.
  3. Put a non stick pan on the stove and add some oil.
  4. Add in the proteins and seasoning.
  5. Stir fry for some minutes until they turn brown
  6. Add in the onions and tomatoes.
  7. Add the tomatoe paste, pepper, seasoning cubes, curry, thyme and salt.
  8. Add the bayleaf, sirfry all for about 7 minutes.
  9. Add in the boiled spag and stir fry the spag on a medium heat for 3 minutes.
  10. Spaghetti is so READY

Tips & Notes

You don't need to add water to the sauce while stir frying, the fresh tomatoes will do the watering.
